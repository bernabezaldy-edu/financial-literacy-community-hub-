## The 5 Building Blocks

### 1. 🛡️ Healthcare
* **Foundation:** A medical emergency can wipe out your savings instantly. Secure reliable short-term and long-term healthcare first.

### 2. 👥 Income Protection (Life Insurance)
* **Protection:** If you are the breadwinner, your family depends on your income. Insurance ensures your loved ones are covered if you disappear too soon.

### 3. 📉 Debt Management
* **Control:** High-interest bad debt is like a leak in your boat. Eliminate it to free up cash flow for building wealth.

### 4. 💰 Emergency Fund
* **Cushion:** Save **3 to 6 months of expenses** in an accessible account so you never have to borrow money for unexpected emergencies.

### 5. 📈 Investments
* **The Roof:** Once your foundation is safe, invest surplus funds into mutual funds or stocks to outpace inflation and grow wealth.
