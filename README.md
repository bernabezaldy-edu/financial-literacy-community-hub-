## 🧱 The 5 Pillars of a Strong Financial Foundation
Building a secure future is exactly like building a house—it must be built from the ground up:
1. **Healthcare** – Your first line of defense against unexpected bankruptcies.
2. **Protection (Life Insurance)** – Immediate income replacement to secure your family.
3. **Debt Management** – Plugging the leaks draining your wealth.
4. **Emergency Fund** – Staying prepared with 3 to 6 months of expenses.
5. **Investments** – Growing your money long-term to outpace inflation.
